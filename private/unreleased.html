ABOUT

Kaiulani is a piano driven singer/songwriter creating a broad range of cinematic, uplifting, thoughtful pop.

Her upcoming release was recorded in Mumbai, India with Sony Music producer Himonshu Parikh, and finished in Los Angeles with producer Julian Feifel.

Influenced by her New York City upbringing, which was infiltrated with hip hop / street music, classical music and musical theatre, Kaiulani has a diverse musical palate rooted in melody, beat and message.
